Get all dogs ->https://dog.ceo/api/breeds/list/all
Get specifc dog image -> https://dog.ceo/api/breed/komondor/images/random

komondor is name of the breed
name of breed comes from "Get all dogs"

Get all dog images of specific breed ->https://dog.ceo/api/breed/komondor/images/
komondor is name of the breed

e.g.
https://dog.ceo/api/breed/bulldog/images/random
https://dog.ceo/api/breed/bulldog/boston/images/random
